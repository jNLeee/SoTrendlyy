Getting Started...

SoTrendly is a one-stop-shop for all of your social media needs. By entering a trend, you will have access to useful information about the trend specific to location, as well as direct access to posts that contain the trend. SoTrendly is currently implemented for Facebook, Twitter, and Youtube so the information provided will be reflective of them.

To get started, simply type in a trend and select a location. SoTrendly will then provide you accurate statistics about that trend in that specific location, polled directly from the provided social media platforms. Below you will find a scrollable list of posts that are associated with the trend. You can click on any of the social media providers to deselect them from the list.
